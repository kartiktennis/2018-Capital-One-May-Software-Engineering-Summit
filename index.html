Twin Peaks West (94127) was seen to have the longest dispatch time out of all the zipcodes in the dataset. In fact, SFPD has already decided to increase patrols in this area after a person was fatally shot in the area.i To reduce reponse times for incidents like this, SFPD should have more units in this area, as it is known to be a crime heavy location.

Lengthy response times in Daly City may largely be due to the fact that there doesnt seem to be a centrally located SFPD station for incidents within the area. In fact, one of the main SFPD stations in San Francisco is the Mission District Police Station (MDPS). Daly City is 18-21 mins away. This most likely serves as the main cause for long response times, which can be mitigated by establishing a central station in the Daly City area.

Again, we see here that Presidio is fairly distant from the MDPS. In fact, the closest police station to the Presidio area is the Northern District Police Station (NDPS), which is still 15 mins away. . The same solution from above can be applied to mitigate reponse times here, that is, placing a more central station the patrol the Presidio Area.
